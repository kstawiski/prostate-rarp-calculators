ISUP Upgrading Prediction

Predict probability of ISUP grade group upgrading from biopsy to radical prostatectomy. Two models: clinic-only (C=0.812) and clinic+MRI (C=0.816).

Model A & B — n=599
🎯

EPE Prediction

Estimate probability of extraprostatic extension using 8 preoperative predictors. LASSO-penalized logistic regression with bootstrap validation (corrected AUC=0.714).

n=492, 174 events

DaVinci vs Versius Dashboard

Interactive results dashboard comparing perioperative outcomes between robotic platforms. Overlap-weighted analysis with falsification testing.

n=602, dual platform
