Research tool — not for clinical decision-making. This calculator implements a model from a single-center consecutive series (n = 604, 492 complete cases). It has undergone bootstrap-corrected internal validation (corrected AUC = 0.714) but not external validation. The model provides moderate discrimination; individual predictions should be interpreted with caution and not replace clinical judgment or established staging tools such as the Partin tables.
